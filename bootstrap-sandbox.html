Lydia's Top Five Albums

My favorite albums of all time, at least for now.

"Get There" by Boa

Boa is my favorite band and this album houses some of my favorite songs of all time.

Listen on Spotify

"Reading, Writing and Arithemetic" by The Sundays

Such a feel-good album for me. Harriet Wheeler's voice is truly something else.

Listen on Spotify

"Ultraviolence (Deluxe)" by Lana Del Rey

I love a lot of Lana's albums, but this one is by far my favorite. I could listen to it in one sitting.

Listen on Spotify

"MELTING MOMENT" by POiSON GiRL FRiEND

This album only has six songs, but it intoduced me to Poison Girl Friend who quickly became one of my favorite artists.

Listen on Spotify

"Twilight" by Boa

Another Boa entry! They are my favorite band, after all. This album has zero skips.

Listen on Spotify
