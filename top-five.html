Lydia's Top Five Albums ★

My favorite albums of all time, at least for now.

  1. "Get There" by Boa Boa is my favorite band and this album houses some of my favorite songs of all time. Jasmine Rodgers is awesome!
  2. "Reading, Writing and Arithmetic" by The Sundays Such a feel-good album for me. Harriet Wheeler's voice is truly something else.
  3. "Ultraviolence" by Lana Del Rey I love a lot of Lana's albums, but this one is by far my favorite. I could listen to it in one sitting.
  4. "MELTING MOMENT" by POiSON GiRL FRiEND This album only has six songs, but it intoduced me to Poison Girl Friend who quickly became one of my favorite artists.
  5. "Blind" by The Sundays Another entry from The Sundays! What can I say, everything they touch is gold.
